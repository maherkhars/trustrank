What the
Score Measures

The TrustRank Score is a composite of verified, tamper-resistant data sources. It cannot be gamed with purchased followers or fake engagement. Every component is on-chain auditable.

  • Real wallet engagement traced back to influencer posts via timestamp correlation
  • Bot detection using behavioral network analysis, not just follower ratios
  • Historical promotions cross-referenced against project outcomes and rug reports
